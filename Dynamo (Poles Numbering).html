Lighting Poles Numbering Sequence

For Overview Explanation: Sample Video

The main idea of the script is to Check the intersaction of your blocks (poles) with your selected polyline (your line) and arrange their order according to their positiob in respected to the start of your line.

  • Selecting related Poles Block through the layer name.
  • Checking the intersaction between your blocks and your selected polyline to set your arranged function action
  • Rearange your poles according to their position along your polyline and create a sequence numbering according to their count.
